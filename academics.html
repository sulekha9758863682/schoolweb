Documents required at the time of registration

Attested photocopy of Date of Birth certificate of the child.
Attested photocopy of Residence proof (either of the following)-
1. Ration Card/Smart Card issued in the name of Parents(Mother/Father having name of child)
2. Domicile Certificate of Child or of his/her Parents.
3. Voter I - Card (EPIC) of any of the Parents.
4. Electricity Bill / MTNL Telephone Bill/Water Bill/Passport in the name of any of the Parents or Child.
5. Aadhaar Card/UID issued in the name of any of the Parents.(Rent agreement will not be considered as proof of residence)
Proof of Sibling (Latest Fees Receipt).
Attested photocopy of SC/ST/OBC Certificate (wherever applicable).
Attested Photocopy of Marksheet of Class X/XII in case of Alumni (wherever applicable).
Note: - All original documents are required to be produced for verification at the time of admission.
