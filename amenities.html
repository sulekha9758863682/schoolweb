FACILITIES


We believe that amenities at school play crucial role in each step of education. Right from enrollment to outstanding performance by all stakeholder while keeping the student at the center. Following these principals we strive to ensure that campus is both aesthetically as well as in term of infrastructure keeps attracting children throughout their term at school.

In this series we ensure that school buildings is striking unified with lush green gardens and lawns. Secondly, classrooms are huge and airy with cross aeration and natural light overlooking gardens. School must have indoor and outdoor playing Courts and sports area along with state-of-the-art Science Lab, Mathematics and Languages Laboratory. Specifically these laboratories must be well-equipped. Considering the hyper-stress developed in the modern competitive world, we stand strong for having meditation centre and yoga centre.

Moreover, to bring and retain more students at school the theme of SWACCH BHARAT MISSION is guiding light for us. Well maintained and ventilated toilets are crucial for attracting and retaining students specially girl students. Similarly, keeping the safety of students on priority we consider cctv facility as well as transportation service as crucial. These give assistance and confidence to parents to keep flag of educating young India high. Further, it is well known fact that basic facilities provided in a school depend on its standards and furthermore on the fee structure. We have been trying our best to deliver maximum from our capacity keeping the idea of educating without profiteering as the main undercurrent. A pictographic glimpse through our initiatives follows here.



Well-Maintained Toilets


Adequate toilet facilities assume great importance in the school premises and they need to be constructed as per the number of students enrolled. In the 21st century, many schools still don’t have an adequate infrastructure that hinders the overall growth of the students. Regular cleaning and washing of the toilets is essential to avoid bacteria and infections.




Ventilation is imperative in every classroom of the school. Students may develop respiratory conditions and find it hard to stay in the classrooms if the ventilation is inappropriate. On top of that, students having pre-existing respiratory conditions can feel more discomfort, which can affect their learning capabilities. Several studies show that students are not able to focus and feel attentive in classrooms where the air quality is poor. Additionally, bacteria, viruses and many other pathogens can also multiply in the school premises that could have negative effects on the health. Thus, in order to offer quality education to students, we provide a healthy and safe environment with proper ventilation.



Space and Size of Classrooms


Overcrowded classrooms often lead to reduced student engagement and improper learning. Teachers also find it difficult to manage an overcrowded class. Every student is not able to get the required attention. So, every classroom have moderate number of students. Additionally, it is spacious enough to offer adequate and personal space for the students.



Playgrounds and Sport Facilities





Open Air Theatre


This is an ideal place for morning assemblies and prayers, as the entire school gathers at a single place. It can also work as an alternative of auditorium and stage to host a function or cultural shows. In case of an emergency, they also can be used as an assembly point for students.



Acoustics and Noise


A classroom equipped with furnished desks and table is a quiet place where the students can have mental peace and they are able to concentrate without any distractions. Noisy classrooms generally have poor effects when it comes to learning.



Lighting


As per an international study, students who get more exposure to natural daylight in the classroom, have more concentration power to grab the learnings. It enhances the morale of the students and teachers that results in quality education. Thus, we have ensured proper access to natural light and avoid artificial lights.



Temperature Control


The classroom’s temperature affects the mood and engagement levels of students and their academic outcomes. If students are feeling too hot or cold, then it directly affects their concentration levels. Proper temperature is maintained through natural and traditional architecture to enable students to focus in a much better way.


Laboratory


It is another must-have facility that is provided in our school. We have distinct sections for physics, chemistry, biology and computer labs. Computer education is essential for today’s generation, so it is available to all the students.
