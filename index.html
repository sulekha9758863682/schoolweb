ABOUT US

Welcome to M L H S S, PURWA MADHDU SINGH,AURAIYA, a HINDI Medium Co-educational Senior Secondary School, that combines the very best in educational practices, powered and propelled by a philosophy of education that is eclectic and yet truly modern. Rated as the most promising UP-Board affiliated school of Central UP, the school offers education to children from Class- I to Senior Secondary level. Well-equipped with modern facilities for all round development of the children, the school is known for class educational practices to ensure that the pupils here get the best possible guidance and assistance in growing up as highly motivated, competitive, and open-minded individuals with Indian values deeply rooted in their hearts. The STUDENTS who leave after years of adventurous and enjoyable schooling, have always been knowledgeable, self- confident and mature individuals. Our team of highly experienced and dynamic teachers believe in imparting knowledge in the unconventional way. Our academic pursuits, along with the range of co-curricular activities, help in honing a child's skills and ensuring that he/she grows to be a mature, responsible citizen.
